an exploration of big data analysis in anthropological drug research

The user-based online organization Erowid aims to provide unbiased information about a broad variety of psychoactive substances. Their website provides pharmacological, chemical, biomedical and botanical resources, and hosts more than 20.000 experience reports written by drug users. This large body of user-based knowledge is frequently consulted by drug users who wish to reduce the harmful effects of drug consumption, and whose primary source of information is more and more online.

In order to understand the knowledge practices associated with Erowid, and their entanglements with contemporary drug use, our team of digital anthropologists explored novel methods to analyze Erowid's digital content and connect it with ethnographic expertise. The results that we present on this website provide both a glimpse into practices of reporting drug use, into actual patterns of drug consumption, and into the possibilities of a digitally literate anthropology.

Dataset description

overview

From a total of 20,534 reports, we consider for the following analysises only the ones concerning a selected set of substances. Reports mentioning the subset constitute roughly the 45% of the full dataset available on Erowid.org.

Dataset breakdown per substance

overview

The visualization shows the number of reports mentioning at least one time one of the selected substances. On a total of 19,168 reports, the biggest amount of reports are related to hallucinogenics (7320), followed by stimulant substances (1332) and sedatives (1309). The smallest amount of reports are related to nootropics and supplements (325).

Users demographics

Overview

The visualization presents some general statistics about users reporting experiences. Even though almost 9.000 (80%) users have reported their body weight, there are some inaccuracies in the weight reporting activity. Average weight is 60 kg. Only 969 reports out of 11,187 are from users who have declared their age. The average user is 18 years old. 9,683 reports out of 11,187 are from users who have declared their sex. There are 1,335 reports from female users and 8,348 from male users .

Language

The text of each report mentioning the same substance has been mined in order to extract relevant language connected to a particualar substance. The visualization shows the top 30 bigrams for each substance and the number of time in which they occur in a subset of reports.
